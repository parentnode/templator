Segments

You can shift segments by adding a ?segment=[segment] parameter to your url.
?segment=desktop
?segment=smartphone
?segment=desktop_light
?segment=seo
?segment=unsupported

You can shift between dev and production includes by adding ?dev=[0|1] to your url.
?dev=1
?dev=0
